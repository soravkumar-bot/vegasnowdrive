Road Trip Essentials

Tips and insights every driver needs before hitting the open road.

🗺️
Plan Your Route
Map your journey ahead of time. Identify fuel stops, charging stations, and scenic detours to make the most of every mile.
🔧
Pre-Trip Checkup
Inspect tires, brakes, oil, and fluids before departure. A quick mechanical check can prevent breakdowns in remote areas.
⚡
EV Charging Strategy
Locate charging stops along your route. Aim to charge between 20–80% for optimal battery health and faster charging speed.
🌤️
Check the Weather
Weather can change road conditions dramatically. Always check forecasts 24–48 hours ahead and adjust plans accordingly.
🎒
Pack Smart
Keep an emergency kit, water, snacks, and a paper map. Less clutter in the car means a safer, more comfortable drive.
📵
Distraction-Free Driving
Use hands-free navigation only. Eyes on the road means arriving safely at every destination on your road trip list.
